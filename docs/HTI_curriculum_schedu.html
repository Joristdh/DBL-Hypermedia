Scheduling 2016 - 2017

This academic year, the courses offered by the Human Technology Interaction group are scheduled as follows.Depending on the choices of your electives and your study progress you might choose to mix the types of courses as listed below in each quartile based on your own situation.

  • Dark blue: mandatory (25 ects courses and 30 ects graduation project)
  • Light blue: core specialisation (10 ects, 2 out of 4)
  • Purple: specialisation electives (15 ects, may also include other core specialisation courses)
  • Pink: free electives (40 ects, of which minimum 15 ects abroad, minimum 15 ects technical (may be abroad))
