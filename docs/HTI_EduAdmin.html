Tasks

The departmental administration office takes care of a number of tasks for all education programs at the faculty Industrial Engineering & Innovation Sciences (IE&IS), including the student administration, keeping up the education information systems, the help desk function for students and teachers, the support of the Exam Committee, and the scheduling of the education programs. Furthermore, Ms. Floris is secretary of the Exam Committee of Innovation Sciences, Ms. Schunselaar is secretary of the Exam Committee of Industrial Engineering. You can thus contact them for all your requests to the Exam Committees. Ms. Sannes and Ms. Zaaijer take care of the class and examination timetables. Ms. Floris is also contacperson for the course descriptions in OWInfo for the education programs.
