Master Human-Technology Interaction

Timeline Master

The master's program has two intake moments. The first quartile for the September intake is Q1, the first quartile for the February intake is Q3.

Please note that you need to register in Osiris for every course you plan to take. Deadlines and procedures can be found here.

Year 1: First quartile

  • Week 1: Information session by Program Chair (dr.ir. Martijn Willemsen)
  • Week 1-2: Submit a list with your mentor preferences to the Program Coordinator (dr. Jaap Hami)
  • Week 3: Mentor assignment completed.You will receive an email from the Program Coordinator.
  • Week 3-4: Make an appointment with your mentor and complete the Broad Diagnostic test on Professional Skills
  • Week 5-6: Meet with your mentor
  • Week 6: Submit the Form Mentor Assignment to the Education Administration
  • Week 6: Submit the Declaration concerning the TU/e Code of Scientific Conduct (in Form01 Mentor Assignment).

Year 1: Remaining quartiles

  • In second quartile: Submit the form for your electives to the Education Administration 
  • In second quartile: Prepare for the international term; information meeting, selection of international courses; check International Course site
  • Third/fourth quartile: First preparation of the master’s thesis project (MTP) including deciding on a potential first supervisor. This could be the mentor, but a student might also decide to do his MTP in another group / with another mentor.

Year 2: First and second quartile

  • International semester
  • Prepare for start of your master thesis project upon return
  • Meet with coordinator graduation projects (Raymond Cuijpers)

Year 2: Third and fourth quartile

  • Master Thesis Project
