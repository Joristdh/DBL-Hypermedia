Professional Skills

In the high-performance workplaces of today you need to be able to present yourself, be creative, show (self) leadership and adopt an entrepreneurial attitude towards your work and your life. Therefore, professional skills such as presentation skills, teamwork skills and writing skills are more important than ever. As a young professional, you need to bring more to the table than only knowledge and a degree. 

The development of professional skills in the master program is supported by an online platform, the so-called TU/e SkillsLab. TU/e SkillsLab is an online environment that gives you easy access to relevant content and tools about a variety of professional skills.

In the TU/e SkillsLab you can take assessments and get feedback whenever you need it. This helps you to know your current level in the various skills.

You can get guidance and coaching to help you setup a personal skill development plan and execute it. You can use the content and tools to practice your skills, on your own time and in your own way. With the TU/e SkillsLab you are in charge of expanding your skills!

Check the presentation and the the video 

As of Monday 31 August, or as soon as you are enrolled, you can login to your personal Skills Assessment page using your TU/e login details. Master students will receive an invitation to their personal Skills assessment page.

More information can be found at the website of the TU/e SkillsLab.
